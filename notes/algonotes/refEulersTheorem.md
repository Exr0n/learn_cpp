# #theorem Euler's Theorem
$$a^{\varphi(n)}\equiv 1 \pmod n$$ iff $$\text{gcd}(a, n) = 1$$

## Consequences
- Fermat's little theorem
- Multiplicative inverse 
- Fast power $a^b \equiv a^b mod \varphi(n)$
